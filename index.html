TradoVault is a free online trading journal and trade tracker built specifically for Indian traders. Whether you trade stocks, crypto, forex, or futures — TradoVault helps you log every trade, analyze your performance, and grow as a trader.

Why Every Trader Needs a Trading Journal

Most traders lose money not because of bad strategies, but because of repeated mistakes and lack of self-awareness. A trading log helps you identify patterns, track your win rate, and improve your risk management discipline over time. Studies show that traders who journal consistently perform significantly better than those who don't.

Key Features of Our Trading Performance Tool

TradoVault is not just a spreadsheet — it's a complete trading performance tool. You get 7 powerful charts including equity curve, win/loss ratio, monthly P&L, and drawdown analysis. Our AI Review feature analyzes your last 20 trades and gives you personalized feedback in both English and Hindi. The Trader Score system rates your discipline, consistency, and risk management on a scale of 0–100.
